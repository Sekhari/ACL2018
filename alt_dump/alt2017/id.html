Important Dates.
Full Paper Submission: 2 June 2017.
Time Zone Info for submissions: You can submit the paper as long as it is 2 June 2017 anywhere in the world.
Author Notification: 24 July 2017.
Camera-Ready Papers Due: 15 August 2017.
Early Registration Deadline: 31 August 2017
Registration by Webpage Deadline: 6 October 2017
Conference: 15 - 17 October 2017.
There is a reception on 14 October 2017.

Proceedings.
The ALT 2017 proceedings will appear in the JMLR Workshop and Conference Proceedings Series.

Special Issue.
Selected papers will be invited for the Special Issue of ALT 2017 in the journal Theoretical Computer Science.

E. M. Gold Award.
One scholarship of EUR 555 will be awarded to a student author of an excellent paper; this paper can be coauthored by other researchers. This year's winner is

Tyler Dohrn for his paper
“The Power of Random Counterexamples”
joint with Dana Angluin.
Congratulations!
The E.M. Gold Award has been awarded since 1999.

Sponsors

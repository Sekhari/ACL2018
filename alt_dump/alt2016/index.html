ALT and DS 2016 are hosted at the University of Bari Aldo Moro and sponsored by Maestra, Cini and Fondazione Puglia.

General chair for ALT 2016 and DS 2016:
Donato Malerba (University of Bari A. Moro)

Programme committee chairs:
Ronald Ortner (Montanuniversität Leoben, Austria)
Hans Ulrich Simon (Ruhr Universität Bochum, Germany)

Local chairs (ALT 2016 and DS 2016):
Annalisa Appice, Corrado Loglisci, Gianvito Pio and Roberto Corrizo (all University of Bari A. Moro).

The 27th International Conference on Algorithmic Learning Theory ALT 2016) and the 19th International Conference on Discovery Science (DS 2016) will be held in Bari, Italy, during 19–21 October 2016.
The host town Bari lies in the east of Italy, see the Wikipedia page on Bari for more information about the conference location.

Programme: The programme is available here; a detailed list of accepted papers is here.

Registration, Accomodation and Venue: Details for the registration are provided on the registration page; the Venue is The Nicolaus Hotel in Bari, see the page on travel information and the DS 2016 webpage for further details.
